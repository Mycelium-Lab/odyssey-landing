How we monetize

Two models, chosen by evidence

Web-to-Web

The entire experience lives on the web. People find us through search or ads, land on a web-native product, and subscribe — no app store, no install friction, and changes that ship in minutes.

It's the fastest way to validate demand and prove economics, which is why most ventures start here.

SubscriptionFreemiumSEO & paidInstant iteration

Web-to-App

A tuned web funnel captures demand and warms the user up, then hands them to a native app built for retention and lifetime value. The web does the selling; the app does the keeping.

We reach for it when value compounds over time — habit products where a home-screen presence is the point.

Native appFunnelsRetentionLTV
How we work

From a signal to a scaling business

1

Signal

A demand signal in one of our verticals becomes a written thesis: who hurts, how badly, and why now. If we can't state it in a sentence, we keep digging.

2

Validate

Landing pages, funnels, and small paid tests measure whether the market actually leans in — before a line of production code exists.

3

Build

A small, senior team ships the core loop in weeks on shared studio infrastructure, then iterates on live user behaviour rather than opinion.

4

Grow

Acquisition, retention, and monetization playbooks turn a working product into a channel that pays for itself.

5

Scale

Ventures that clear their retention and economics gates get full resources; the rest are pivoted or retired without drama.
