Life at the studio

Small team, real ownership

Odyssey is deliberately small. That's not a stage we're trying to grow out of — it's the point. Small teams mean your work is visible, your decisions matter, and the distance between an idea and a shipped feature is measured in days.

You won't sit in a queue behind three layers of approval. You'll own a slice of a real product, talk to real users, and see your work in the metrics by the end of the week. When something works, everyone knows it was you. When it doesn't, we learn and move — no blame, no theatre.

We care about outcomes, not hours. Work from where you do your best thinking, on a schedule that fits your life. What we ask in return is simple: take ownership, move fast, and tell the truth about what the numbers say.

How we hire

A short, honest process

No trick questions, no month-long gauntlets. We want to see how you think and how you build — fast.

1

Intro call

A relaxed conversation about your work, what you want to build, and whether the studio is the right fit for both sides.

2

Deep dive

We go deep on real work — yours and ours. Expect to talk through decisions, trade-offs, and how you'd approach a live problem.

3

Build together

A short, paid, practical exercise close to what you'd actually do here — so you can feel the work and we can see it.

4

Decision

A clear yes or no, quickly, with real feedback either way. No ghosting — you'll always know where you stand.
