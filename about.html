Our story

Idea to global launch

Odyssey operates across two formats: Web-to-Web (W2W) — fully web-native products with subscription or freemium monetization — and Web-to-App (W2A) — optimized web funnels that convert audiences directly into mobile app installs.

We move fast, validate early, and double down on what works. Winning products get full resources; underperformers are pivoted or retired quickly. That discipline is how a small team ships products built for Tier-1 markets from day one.

Odyssey is a brand operated by Baltic Mobile OÜ, headquartered in Tallinn, Estonia.

The studio model

Three ways to build. We chose the hardest one.

Agencies bill. Funds bet. A studio builds, owns, and operates — here's the difference at a glance.

The agency

Builds for clients

Ships someone else's roadmap and gets paid for the output. When the project ends, it hands over the keys and moves to the next brief — with no stake in what happens after launch.

Owns the invoice, not the outcome.

The fund

Bets on founders

Buys a slice of someone else's execution and watches from the board seat. Real upside when it works — but the hands on the product are never its own.

Owns a slice, not the steering wheel.

The Odyssey — venture studio

Owns the whole journey

Conceives the idea, builds the product, runs the growth, and operates the business. We do the work and hold the outcome — there's no one else to hand the keys to.

Owns the work, the risk, and the result.

That difference shapes everything else: shared infrastructure instead of one-off builds, patient operating instead of quick exits, and lessons that stay in the house instead of leaving with the client.

Where we're headed

The next chapter

Our ambition is simple to state and hard to earn: build a portfolio of consumer products that reach millions of people across Tier-1 markets, each solving a real problem well enough that people pay to keep it.

In the near term that means going deeper in the four verticals we know — Health & Wellness, Legaltech, Education, and Entertainment — and sharper on the two models we run, Web-to-Web and Web-to-App. Over time it means a compounding studio: every product we ship makes the next one faster to build and cheaper to grow.

The map gets drawn one shipped product at a time — and we intend to keep drawing.
